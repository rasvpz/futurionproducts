What Tests We Offer?

Surge Comparison Test

This test finds faults and damages in the coils, windings, compressors, and alternators. It is also used in testing faulty connections, wrong turn counts, and DC motor faults. Our experts perform surge comparison tests following safety protocols to ensure the integrity of the equipment and usually apply such tests for the transformer and generator repair.

Insulation Resistance Test

Insulation deteriorates over time due to the worst temperatures, chemical abrasion, or being gnawed by rats. An insulation resistance test determines the level of damage to the insulation, which, if remains unattended, can prove fatal to power reliability and personnel safety.

Polarity Index Test

Polarization index (PI) testing is a variation of the insulation resistance test that checks for specific concerns in a motor, such as moisture and insulation breakdown. To avoid unexpected downtime and costly repair due to damage, certain tests should be conducted for optimum performance.

Static Test

For a motor to continue safe and successful operation, periodic static testing is done to predict the potential of the motor. This test is most effective in allowing the tracking and trending of the working condition of an electric motor on a regular basis.

Megger Test

This test is performed using an insulation tester resistance meter to understand the condition of the insulation. It is necessary to check the health of insulation periodically as it is prone to get damaged due to environmental, chemical, and other factors.
